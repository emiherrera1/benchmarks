What is the Creator Efficiency Score?

The CES measures how efficiently you're converting your audience into revenue while managing your costs. It's the industry standard for evaluating creator business performance.

CES Formula

CES = (Revenue Growth / Monthly Burn) × (Revenue per Audience Member) × 100
Revenue Growth: $0 This month - Last month
Monthly Burn: $0 Operating costs
Revenue per Audience: $0 Total revenue ÷ audience size
